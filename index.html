About PSE

India's Premier Platform for School Admissions

Premier Schools Exhibition is in its 22nd consecutive year, spanning across cities in India and international locations. It brings India's legacy Boarding Schools & New-Age International and Day Schools — across all boards — on one platform to help parents choose the right school.

The exhibition has reached 12 countries and 17 cities, enhancing student diversity in Indian schools through international admissions and opening opportunities for schools to attract talent from across the world.
